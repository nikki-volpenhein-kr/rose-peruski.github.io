Introducing Snack Talks

  • According to an article from the Alliance for Academic Internal Medicine, doctors Avraham Z. Cooper, MD and Jeremy B. Richards, MD, MA stated, “adult learning data suggest that typical learner attention span wanes after about 15 to 20 minutes.”
  • The snack talk accomplishes multiple things
    • Keeps attendees engaged
    • Maximizes learning
    • Allows time for office hours
    • Smaller time ask for live viewers and those watching the recording
  • "Snack Talk": A bite-sized piece of information that takes as long to consume as your afternoon snack

The Snack Talk Format

  • Stay on a single topic:
    • Stays within time constraints
    • Can go into appropriate depth without losing participants
  • Go back to the source: we often use HTML pages to explain the connection between what we see and what AT understands

Bite #1: Make It Fun & Genuine

We all have different definitions of fun, so be creative and true to your own personality.

Being genuine and up front about what we know and don’t know has aided in success.

Fun

Rose still has an undying love for The Office, so many examples center around that as an example.

    Why?
  • No one feels that their product is singled out
  • The Office is widely known and generally not controversial
  • The Office is easy to riff off of to bring in moments of laughter or smiles

Genuine

Our sessions and recordings are by no means perfect. The struggles we go through when an AT doesn’t do what we expect (despite all the practicing we do beforehand) is relatable and puts people at ease that if they are having trouble it isn’t because they are incapable of learning a11y or the tools, but that it isn’t always easy and clear cut. We tell people “progress over perfection” and it rings true when we are demonstrating that ourselves.

Bite #2: Bring Others In

  • We have found that encouraging others' contribution to the conversation builds accessibility culture
  • Examples:
    • Audit Help
    • UX Writing & Alt Tags
    • Design System Partnership

Bite #3: Make It Available

  • Snack talks are recorded and uploaded into Sharepoint/Teams
  • Promote that it was uploaded afterward.
  • Other promotion ideas: share links in newsletter, email blast, etc.
